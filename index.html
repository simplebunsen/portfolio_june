About me

Lukas (20)

hat Weitsicht :)

Hello, world.

Media and Tech always fascinated me.
Starting in childhood, when I was shooting blurry pictures with my parents' digital camera, trying (unsuccessfully) to make custom levels for Super Mario 64, or designing a logo for my mum's music club, a passion was ignited.
Later on, when the stresses of school started to weigh down on me, I found catharsis in creating silly YouTube videos (long since deleted!) and trying to attract people's attention to game modifications I had created.
In my adult life, I like to try new things, both tech and creativity related. While I do complete courses and watch tutorials, I've found that learning-by-doing helps me to learn new topics very efficiently. Hence you'll find many projects that I've completed on my journey in the showcase below!
Thanks for visiting my webpage!
